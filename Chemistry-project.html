Harmful effects of air pollutants

  • Carbon Monoxide | Cause breathing difficulties and even death
  • Methane | Is a greenhouse gas, which causes global warming
  • Unburnt Hydrocarbons | Cause caner and react with nitrogen oxides to form ozone
  • Ozone | Forms photochemical smog, which irritates the eyes and lungs and causes breathing difficulties. It also damages crops
  • Sulfur Dioxide and Oxides Of Nitrogen | Irritates the eyes and lungs and causes breathing difficulties. It also react with water in the atmosphere to form acid rain, which corrodes buildings and harms aquatic life
